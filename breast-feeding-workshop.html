Breastfeeding Benefits Beyond the Bond

Aura Breastfeeding Online Workshop
Who fed me from her gentle breast And hushed me in her arms to rest, And on my cheek sweet kisses prest? "My Mother.”

As comforting and healthy as breastfeeding can be, it is not always easy in the first few weeks while recovering from birth. With the information in this class, you can prepare to get breastfeeding off to a great start and look forward to the many benefits that breastfeeding can provide to you and your baby.

Benefits of Breastfeeding

  • As you probably know, breastfeeding provides countless benefits to you, your baby, and your family. For the vast majority of babies, breast milk is the healthiest choice.
  • There is a great deal of evidence that not breastfeeding increases the risks of many childhood diseases including: diabetes, obesity, asthma, ear infections, digestive disorders, and SIDS.
  • Mothers who do not breastfeed are at higher risk of postpartum hemorrhage, Type 2 diabetes, breast cancer, ovarian cancer, and postpartum depression.
  • Breastfed babies are healthier in the first year than formula-fed babies, and the health benefits continue for as long as they are breastfed, and beyond.

Who can attend the Breastfeeding Care workshop?

Pregnant Mothers or Individuals from 7+ Months are eligible to attend this workshop.

What Our Breastfeeding Basics Workshop Provides?

The Workshop will help familiarize you with other important information to better prepare you for life after baby, like how to know if your baby is gaining weight normally or get ready to return to work in the phase of your breastfeeding. Through Evidence based information, you will know how to identify and respond to feeding cues, manage nighttime feedings, and how you naturally bond with baby through breastfeeding.
