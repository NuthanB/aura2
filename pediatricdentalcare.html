Online Pediatric Dental Consultation at Aura

At Aura, we believe that a healthy smile begins from the very start. Our Online Pediatric Dental Consultation service is designed to support parents and caregivers at every stage of a child’s oral development - from newborn care to toddler routines and beyond.

Led by the experienced Dr. Preeti Jha, a specialist in Pediatric and Preventive Dentistry, her one-on-one virtual consultations provide personalized advice, preventive care strategies, and practical guidance tailored to your child’s unique needs.

Dr. Preeti is a highly skilled and compassionate pediatric dentist, specializing in the field of Pediatric and Preventive Dentistry. She completed her post-graduation in this field from Pandit B.D Sharma University of Health Sciences, Rohtak, Haryana. Throughout her academic career, Dr. Jha demonstrated exceptional proficiency and was honored with the prestigious Student Plaque Award by the International College of Dentists for her outstanding performance in her Bachelor of Dental Surgery (B.D.S.).

In addition to her clinical skills, Dr. Jha is known for her gentle and empathetic approach, particularly in managing anxious children. Through specialized behavior management techniques, she ensures that every child leaves the clinic with a smile, making dental visits a positive experience.

With over 13 years of experience in pediatric dentistry, Dr. Preeti Jha is well-versed in a wide range of dental procedures, including: -

  • Pulpotomies
  • Crowns
  • Interceptive Orthodontics
  • Restorations
  • Extractions
  • Apexification and Apexogenesis
  • Full Mouth Rehabilitation under General Anesthesia

Scope of Services for Online Pediatric Dental Consultation

  • Infant and Children Oral Health: Guidance on infant oral care, teething, pacifier/thumb-sucking habits, and early dental development. Includes tips for brushing, fluoride use, and cavity prevention and treatment guidance for several oral diseases.
  • Parental Support and Prenatal Education: Offering advice for expecting mothers on prenatal oral health, newborn oral care, and addressing issues like tongue-tie and lip-tie.
  • Dental Consultations for Adults: Personalised advice and support for adults regarding their individual dental health concerns and conditions.
