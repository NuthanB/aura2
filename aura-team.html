Meet the Team

Celebrating Parenthood through Informed and Supported Journeys

  • Shree LP

    Founder & Educator

    Shree LP, Founder & Director of Aura, is an internationally certified Pregnancy Fitness Educator, Childbirth Educator. As a mother, she is passionate about inspiring expectant parents through knowledge and education. Featured on various platforms, Shree has transformed lives globally with her expertise. She was recognized as ‘The Most Inspiring Woman, 2022’ by She Magazine for her exceptional contributions to pregnancy fitness and childbirth education.

  • Shreya Abraham

    Prenatal and Postnatal Fitness Instructor

    I'm Shreya Abraham, a proud mother to a one-year-old daughter and a certified Prenatal Yoga and Fitness Instructor. My fitness journey started early as a district-level athlete and school throwball player. At 14, I incorporated Yoga into my daily routine, with Surya Namaskara and Pranayama becoming favorites. Alongside fitness, I am a Carnatic singer, certified makeup artist, and model, blending artistry and wellness throughout my life. I am proud to foster a community where our approach builds trust and a sense of belonging, empowering each mother to feel connected and supported.
