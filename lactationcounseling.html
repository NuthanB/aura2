Lactation Consultation

At Aura, we offer expert lactation support to guide and empower mothers through every stage of their breastfeeding journey. Whether you're a first-time mom or facing new challenges, our consultations provide personalized, compassionate, and evidence-based care.

Our lactation services are guided by Soumya Hiremath, a certified lactation consultant with a B.Sc. in Nutrition and ACLP certification, and former experience at Motherhood Hospital. With a deep understanding of maternal and infant nutrition, she brings both clinical expertise and empathy to every consultation.

Scope of Practice

A comprehensive approach that addresses a wide range of breastfeeding-related needs, including:

  • Latching difficulties
  • Low milk supply
  • Nipple pain and soreness
  • Engorgement and blocked ducts
  • Feeding positions and techniques
  • Breastfeeding challenges for preterm or special-needs infants
  • Transitioning back to work while breastfeeding
  • Weaning and extended breastfeeding support

Our Services

  • Individual Lactation Sessions
    One-on-one support to help you overcome breastfeeding concerns and build confidence in your feeding journey. Available in-person or online.
  • Postnatal Breastfeeding Counselling
    Support during the early postpartum period to help establish a healthy breastfeeding routine and address common early challenges.
  • Breastfeeding Support Up to 2 Years
    Continued guidance for mothers breastfeeding into toddlerhood, including follow-ups, weaning strategies, and lifestyle support.
  • Home Visits (In Select Locations)
    On-site support in the comfort of your home to assess feeding, positioning, and baby’s latch with personalized care.

Consult our expert lactation counselor for personalized support!
