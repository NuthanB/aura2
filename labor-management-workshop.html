Aura labor Management Workshop
Get prepared and informed for a safe and healthy birth

"Giving Birth can be a transformational experience. Supported birth wants it to be an empowering and positive one".

With all of the experiences that pregnancy brings, labor often makes expecting parents the most nervous. Know you are in good company if you have many questions about labor, and think of it as an important step toward welcoming your new baby into the world.

The Workshop that empowers couples with knowledge, confidence and support to make conscious choices about the birth of their baby.

The class is based on a combination of technical knowledge, confidence in the natural process of birthing, rediscovering and trusting instincts and many time-proven pain coping tools and techniques. Our Birth Classes at Aura are eclectic and acknowledge the importance of birth environment, labor, support, and collaborative "methods".

Who can attend the Labor Management workshop?

Pregnant Couples or Individuals from 7+ Months are eligible to attend this workshop online or ar our centre.

What will you learn in the Labor Management Workshop?

  • Healthy Birth Practices
  • Birth Hormones
  • Stages of Labor
  • Perineal Massage
  • Labor Positions
  • Comfort Coping Techniques for Labor
  • Partner’s Role and Support to Mothers
  • Medical Interventions
  • The Golden Hour

Benefits of attending Labor Management Workshops

Learning helpful labor positions and strategies to promote labor progress will allow your body to work with your baby toward a safe and healthy birth.

The prior knowledge on childbirth helps to understand your body’s ability to give birth. The class gives confidence to the couples to tackle the Third Trimester phase of pregnancy and childbirth, which is one of life's most exciting events with joy be it Natural Birth or C-Section.

Drawing from the best research evidence, Aura Childbirth education classes focus on not only hands-on learning but essential information that will help you push for the best care for you and your family.
